WE HAVE GAME AWARDS 2024 NOMINEES

The Game Awards 2024 has announced its nominees for Game of the Year (GOTY), and it's a stacked list featuring some of the most anticipated and critically acclaimed titles of the year. Here are the nominees:

  • Astro Bot (Team ASOBI/SIE)
  • Balatro (LocalThunk/Playstack)
  • Black Myth: Wukong (Game Science)
  • Elden Ring: Shadow of the Erdtree (FromSoftware/Bandai Namco)
  • Final Fantasy VII Rebirth (Square Enix)
  • Metaphor: ReFantazio (Studio Zero/Atlus/Sega)

This year's nominees showcase a diverse range of genres and styles, from platformers to action-RPGs to JRPGs. Several of these games have already received critical acclaim for their innovative gameplay, stunning visuals, and compelling stories.

It's worth noting that Final Fantasy VII Rebirth and Astro Bot lead the pack with seven nominations each, making them the most-nominated titles of the year. The Game Awards 2024 will take place on December 12th, where the winners of each category will be announced.
